Outsourced Immigration Services

At Luke Bowman Law, our goal is to provide efficient and streamlined services to each of our clients, regardless of case complexity. For many of our clients, this requires a hands-on approach and we accomplish it through our full-service option. We provide a complete, custom package for each of our clients. We learn your business, get to know your current practices, and then devise a solution that is quickly and easily implemented.

Our objective is to let your team focus on strategic initiatives and other functions rather than administrative tasks. A solid, skilled partner for an immigration program is critical to resource development, employee satisfaction/retention, immigration compliance and improving the overall employee experience by providing support for the following:

  • Ensure clear cut procedures and processes
  • Provide employee satisfaction through case tracking, high level of reachability and communication and easy to use tools
  • Track deadlines and critical dates
  • Streamlined processing
  • Maintain best practices and follow through with immigration compliance

Some of the services we provide are as below:

  • I-9 compliance
  • Validate work authorization for new hires and existing employees
  • Work closely with designated managers and employees for visa application process
  • Focus on employee communication and respond on a timely basis (within 24 hours)
  • Manage Visa Process
    • Initiate new cases (H1’s, L1’s, TN’s, H1B cap etc.)
    • Maintain information and initiate dependent’s visa processing and extensions on a timely basis
    • Assist with H4 EAD and L2 EAD process
    • Initiate Green Card Process
    • Maintain expiration dates for all types of visa-based employees and initiate extensions and amendments on time
    • Assist with stamping process in the home country
    • Assist with business visa process
    • DS-160 form
    • Assist with scheduling consulate interviews
    • Assist with consulate and border entry related questions
    • Assist with business visa process
    • Post LCA’s
    • Maintain Public Access Files
    • Assist with client letter drafts
  • Prepare corporate policies and programs to ensure full compliance is met with federal laws and regulations adhering to state work site enforcement laws
  • Assist with immigration officer site visits
  • Represent employers who face investigations, enforcement-related litigation and audits
  • Assist with client letter drafts
  • Assist with immigration related reports
  • Manage all the documents in a single, secure location, eliminating risk and redundancy. Reduce bottlenecks through centralized storage and eliminate the need for paper files and ensure audit readiness
  • Elevate data security and protect sensitive employee information
  • Assist with immigration related reports
  • Assist with maintaining and forecasting immigration related budget
