NONIMMIGRANT VISAS

The Law Office of Luke Bowman & Associates provides assistance and advice on all types of Non Immigrant Visas as mentioned below. We also provide guidance for individuals with prior denials resulting from immigration violations.


  • B-1/B-2 (Visitors for work or pleasure)
  • E-1/E-2 (Treaty Traders and Investors)
  • E-3 (Professionals from Australia)
  • H-1B (Professional positions requiring at least a bachelor’s degree)
  • L-1A/L-1B (Intracompany Transferees – Managers and those in Specialized Knowledge positions), L blanket application
  • O-1 (Individuals of Extraordinary Ability in the Sciences, Business and the Performing Arts)
  • TN-1/TN-2 (Professionals from Canada and Mexico under NAFTA)
  • F-1

EMPLOYMENT AUTHORIZATION DOCUMENTS

We realize that immigration law can be stressful and confusing. Our experienced team of attorneys and legal analysts will help you explore different options to work and live in the US. For some foreign nationals, this includes employment authorization documents (EAD).


  • H4 EAD
  • L2 EAD
  • GC EAD

DEPENDENTS VISA

At Luke Bowman Law, we focus our practice on helping families navigate the immigration landscape. Our team will evaluate your case and process dependent visas that can help you and your family members remain in the United States. To find out more about the Dependent visa application process and requirements, contact our offices for a consultation.


  • TD - For Dependents of TN visa holders
  • L2 - For dependents of L1 visa holders
  • TD - For Dependents of TN visa holders

CORPORATE COMPLIANCE

At Luke Bowman Law, we assist companies in developing comprehensive systems to ensure compliance with U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services, Department of Labor, and State Department regulations. In addition, our attorneys conduct audits for businesses to identify immigration violations, minimize the potential liability for past violations, and train staff to prevent future problems.


  • I-9 Audits & training
  • Public Access File Audits
  • Due Diligence
  • Immigration Training
  • Mandatory immigration compliance files

FAMILY-BASED IMMIGRATION

Family is important and you do not want to be separated from your loved ones. We help you prevent or minimize separation of family members. We assist couples with marriage-based immigration and help clients with relatives living abroad to plan the best reunion strategy. For those who have gained permanent residency, we can help assess whether naturalization is a desirable step.


  • K-1/K-2/K-3/K-4
  • Family Based I-130 filings
  • Adjustment of Status
  • Consular Processing

IMMIGRANT (GREEN CARD)

If you are an immigrant seeking permanent residency in the United States, Luke Bowman Law can assist you. Our attorneys can communicate in an easy-to-understand way exactly what needs to be done to help you achieve your dreams of permanent residency


  • PERM (EB2 and EB3)
  • National Interest Waivers(NIW)
  • Outstanding Researcher(EB1B)
  • Extraordinary Ability (EB1A)
  • Immigrant petitions based on approved PERMs (EB2 and EB3)
  • Immigrant Investor Program (EB-5) – This program provides an opportunity for successful entrepreneurs to invest in U.S. based businesses creating jobs for US based individuals, while pursuing permanent residency process for themselves and their immediate family members.
  • Adjustment of Status
  • Consular Processing
  • Intracompany Transfer Managers or Executives (EB1C)

NATURALIZATION AND CITIZENSHIP

The application and interview process for U.S. citizenship can be complicated and time consuming. We will help you navigate the complex requirements and procedural rules of the application process and prepare for your naturalization interview. Our attorneys understand the difficulties presented by life in a foreign country, therefore, we are happy to take the time to explain the intricacies of U.S. immigration law and explain the naturalization process.


  • Naturalization application
  • Derivative citizenship
  • Passport application
  • Reentry permits
  • False claim of United States citizenship
  • Military naturalization
  • Appeal of denial of naturalization
  • Judicial review of naturalization
  • Denaturalization
